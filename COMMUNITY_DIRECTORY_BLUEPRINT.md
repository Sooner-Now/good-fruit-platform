# Good Fruit Community Directory Blueprint

Good Fruit is growing toward a trusted local directory and interactive community platform. These are the core data areas and tools we should prepare for.

## Directory Records

- Churches and ministries
- Community groups
- Businesses and service providers
- Volunteers and mentors
- Prayer requests and answered prayer updates
- Needs, offers, barter, and free goods
- Events, trainings, projects, and service opportunities
- Courses, certifications, and learning pathways

## Trust Levels

- Visitor: email-confirmed browsing and search.
- Guest: ID-verified interaction, posting, responding, team joining, and marketplace introductions.
- Member: deeper trust history, orchard participation, mentoring, leading, and bonus eligibility.
- Business: paid membership, verified marketplace profile, offers, services, and participation in member bonus distributions.

## AI Advocate Responsibilities

- Help users understand what they can do at their current trust level.
- Recommend prayer, learning, service, and community next steps.
- Match people with needs to people with means.
- Encourage Scripture-rooted growth without replacing pastors, counselors, doctors, or emergency services.
- Escalate crisis, abuse, exploitation, or urgent safety concerns to human help.
- Protect sensitive documents, private prayer requests, and vulnerable users.

## Near-Term Build Order

1. Add a real backend for accounts and directory records.
2. Add email confirmation and role-based access.
3. Add secure identity verification for Guest and above.
4. Add admin review tools for posts, needs, listings, and reports.
5. Add searchable directory pages for churches, groups, businesses, courses, and events.
6. Connect the Advocate to the live directory and user profile.
7. Add payment and business membership workflows.
8. Add impact tracking for prayer, needs fulfilled, hours served, and learning completed.
